================================================================================
1. SERVICE DESCRIPTION
================================================================================

Tabeza POS Connect is a Windows background service that:
- Monitors your Windows print spooler for receipt data
- Captures receipt information AFTER printing completes
- Sends receipt data to Tabeza cloud services for digital receipt delivery
- Does NOT interfere with or delay your normal printing operations

IMPORTANT: Your printer and POS system operate independently. Tabeza POS Connect 
only observes print jobs after they complete - it never blocks or modifies 
printing.

4. DATA COLLECTION AND PRIVACY
================================================================================

4.1 WHAT WE COLLECT

Tabeza POS Connect collects and transmits:
- Receipt data: items, quantities, prices, totals, taxes
- Venue identifier (Bar ID)
- Timestamp of each transaction
- Device identifier (anonymous)
- Print job metadata

4.2 WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT

We do NOT collect:
- Customer names or personal information from receipts
- Credit card numbers or payment card data
- Employee personal information
- Any data unrelated to receipts

4.3 HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

Receipt data is used to:
- Provide digital receipts to your customers via the Tabeza app
- Enable customers to link receipts to their tabs
- Generate analytics and reports for your venue
- Improve receipt parsing accuracy

4.4 DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

- Receipt data is encrypted during transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Data is stored securely on Supabase cloud infrastructure
- Access is restricted to authorized Tabeza systems and your venue staff
- We implement industry-standard security practices

4.5 DATA RETENTION

- Raw receipt data: Retained for 90 days
- Parsed receipt data: Retained for 1 year or as required by law
- You may request deletion of your venue's data at any time

4.6 DATA SHARING

We do NOT sell your data. We may share data only:
- With your explicit consent
- With service providers who assist in operating Tabeza (under strict NDAs)
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect Tabeza's rights or prevent fraud
